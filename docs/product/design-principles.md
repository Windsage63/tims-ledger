# Design Principles

## Product Name

**Windsage Ledger**

## Tagline

**Simple books I can understand.**

## UX Direction

Windsage Ledger should feel modern, calm, and purpose-built. It should not feel like a generic SaaS dashboard, a tax product, or a spreadsheet wearing a new coat.

## Principles

1. **Workflows before dashboards**

   The first screen should help the user act: enter time, review expenses, build invoices, apply payments, and resolve balances.

2. **Records over formulas**

   The UI should make records and relationships visible: time to invoice, expense to invoice, payment to invoice, credit to customer.

3. **Dense but readable**

   Tables should support scanning, sorting, filtering, and review without oversized marketing-style cards.

4. **Explain balances**

   Customer balances should always be explainable from invoices, payments, applications, and credits.

5. **Automation with review**

   OCR and imports should suggest data, not silently approve it. The user should remain in control of accounting records.

6. **Print still matters**

   Generated invoices and reports should look professional on paper and PDF, not just on screen.

7. **Local ownership**

   The app should make it obvious where the business data lives and how to back it up.
