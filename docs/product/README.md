# Product Notes

This folder captures product workflow, UX, screen planning, and design decisions for Windsage Ledger.

The app should be designed around the real work:

  - entering time
  - entering and categorizing expenses
  - attaching receipts
  - building invoices from approved source records
  - applying payments and advances
  - understanding customer balances
  - exporting reports for bookkeeping and tax review

The Stitch files in `references/stitch/` are inspiration only. They are useful for mood and layout exploration, but the production app should use a denser, clearer design tailored to Air Advantage's actual workflow.
